DATASET USED

This dataset contains information on IT salaries in the United States. It includes the following features

  • Job title: The job title of the IT professional.
  • Company: The company that the IT professional works for.
  • Location: The location of the IT professional's job.
  • Salary: The salary of the IT professional..
  • Years of experience: The number of years of experience the IT professional has..

The dataset was created by Taufiq Hidayat, and it is available on Kaggle. It is a valuable resource for anyone who is interested in learning more about IT salaries in the United States. Here are some of the things you can do with this dataset: Analyze the salaries of IT professionals in different job titles. Compare the salaries of IT professionals in different companies.
